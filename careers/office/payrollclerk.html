Payroll Clerk

Forest Park, GA

Job Description:

The payroll clerk position is accountable for collecting timekeeping information, incorporating a variety of deductions into a periodic payroll, and issuing pay and pay-related information to employees.

Duties:

  • Data entry
  • Verify timekeeping records.
  • Record payroll data in our software system and verify all amounts prior to cutting checks.
  • Initiate direct deposits.
  • Change employee banking records when necessary to process payments accurately.
  • Maintain compliant policies and procedures for processing payroll check.

Requirements:

  • Minimum one year payroll department experience.
  • Ability to process basic functions and formulas in Microsoft Excel.
  • Familiarity with payroll software a plus.
  • Strong attention to detail required.

Education:

  • High school diploma/GED required .
  • Bachelor’s Degree in Accounting or related field preferred.
